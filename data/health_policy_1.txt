Health Insurance Policy - Comprehensive Coverage

This health insurance policy provides comprehensive coverage for hospitalization due to illness or accidental injury. The policy covers up to 10 lakhs per year for hospitalization expenses including room charges, doctor fees, and medication.

Key Benefits:
- Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals
- Pre and post hospitalization coverage (30 days pre, 60 days post)
- Day care procedures coverage
- Ambulance charges up to Rs. 2,000 per hospitalization

Exclusions:
- Pre-existing conditions for first 3 years
- Cosmetic surgery
- Dental treatment (except due to accident)
- Mental health treatment

Validity: 3 years from policy start date
Premium: Rs. 15,000 per year
