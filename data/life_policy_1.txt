Life Insurance Policy - Term Life Coverage

This term life insurance policy provides financial protection to the family in case of the policyholder's untimely death. The policy offers coverage of Rs. 50 lakhs with a term of 30 years.

Key Benefits:
- Death benefit payout to nominated beneficiaries
- Tax benefits under Section 80C and 10(10D)
- Option to convert to permanent life insurance
- No maturity benefit (pure protection)

Exclusions:
- Death due to suicide within first year
- Death due to war or terrorism
- Death while committing crime

Validity: 30 years from policy start date
Premium: Rs. 8,000 per year
