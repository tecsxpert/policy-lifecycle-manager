Vehicle Insurance Policy - Comprehensive Auto Coverage

This comprehensive vehicle insurance policy covers damage to the insured vehicle due to accident, theft, or natural calamities. The policy also includes third-party liability coverage as required by law.

Key Benefits:
- Own damage coverage up to Rs. 10 lakhs
- Third-party liability coverage (unlimited for injury/death, Rs. 7.5 lakhs for property damage)
- No claim bonus up to 50%
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Cashless repair at network garages

Exclusions:
- Normal wear and tear
- Mechanical breakdown
- Damage due to racing or speed testing
- Damage while driving under influence

Validity: 1 year from policy start date
Premium: Rs. 12,000 per year (varies by vehicle value and location)
